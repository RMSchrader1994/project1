"The first game in the series introduces Gabriel Knight, a financially struggling horror novelist based in New Orleans. Gabriel is following a series of homicides, dubbed "The Voodoo Murders" for their apparent voodoo overtones hoping to use the police investigation, led by Detective Mosely, as the basis of a new novel. However, as he draws deeper into the investigation, and his shop assistant Grace Nakimura performs historical research for him, Gabriel links the murder spree both to New Orleans' voodoo past and towards the beautiful and mysterious New Orleans socialite, Malia Gedde."

Characters

Gabriel Knight

Actor: Tim Curry

A struggling author and rare bookstore owner following the recent string of murders dubbed the voodoo murders in hopes of basing his next novel on the case. As the case progresses though, Gabriel discovers his nightmares and family are tightly linked to the case.

Tetelo

Actress: Linda Gary

Former lover of Gabriel’s ancestor, Gunther, who was betrayed. Now she possesses her female ancestors with the hope of destroying the schattenjager line for good and make New Orleans her domain.

Malia Gette

Actress: Leilani Jones

A wealthy Africa-American socialite, lover of Gabriel, and ancestor of Tetelo who is used as a vessel for the angered loa. She wishes for a normal life, but her family obligations deny her of that chance.

Grace Nakimura

Actress: Leah Remini

A recent graduate with a masters in history and plans for a phd. As Gabriel’s bookshop assistant she assists him with any research he needs during the case. Towards the end of the case she is kidnapped and the rescued by Gabriel.

Franklin Mosely

Actor: Mark Hamill

A New Orleans police detective with hopes of sometime becoming police chief and Gabriel’s childhood best friend. He reopens the voodoo murders case at Gabriel behest and gets dragged into the real voodoo of New Orleans.

Wolfgang Ritter

Acotr: Efrem Zimbalist Jr.

Gabriel’s Grandfather’s brother and only remaining schattenjager remaining rittersburg Germany. Assists gabriel in his case and sacrifices himself to see gabriel become the last remaining schattejagger.

Doctor John

Actor: Michael Dorn

The curator of New Orlean’s voodoo museum and tour guide of St. Louis Cementary. He is also the right hand man of Malia/Tetelo in the cartel. He is killed by Mosely during the final confrontation of the game.

Gran

Actress: Linda Gary

Gabriel’s grandmother and sole guardian after his parent’s deaths when he was 8.

Professor Hartridge

Actor: Monte Markham

An English professor at Tulane who lectures on African history, mainly voodoo culture from Africa during the game. The cartel has him killed after he begin researching for Gabriel.

Gerde Hull

Actress: Mary Key Bergman

A maid and close friend of Wolfgang at Schloss Ritter.

Crash

Actor: Christopher Lytton

A secret runner for the cartel who Mosely interrogates as part of the voodoo murders investigation. The day he is related the cartel has him killed while speaking with Gabriel.

Madame Cazaunoux

Actress: Susan Silo

A New Orleans elitist with strong family ties that can be traced back to 1700. Her appearance as a good christian girl mask her strong belief in the voodoo that controls the city.
