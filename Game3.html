"Four years after the events of the second game, Gabriel and Grace are asked by the exiled Prince of Albany to protect his newborn son from a centuries-old family threat that appears to be a group of vampires. Shortly afterwards, the boy is kidnapped and Gabriel follows the kidnappers to the mysterious French village of Rennes-le-Château. Gabriel's arrival coincides with that of a tour group whose members are all supposedly in town hunting for a legendary local treasure linked to the Knights Templar, the Cathars and potentially connected to the Holy Grail."

Characters

Gabriel Knight

Actor: Tim Curry

Continuing his duties as sole Schattenjager his current case involves reducing the kidnapped infant of Prince James by mysterious night walkers(vampires). Throughout the case Gabriel learns just how his family became Schattenjagers.

Excelsior Montreaux

Actor: John de Lancie

A French vineyard owner, Montreaux is secretly the leader of the vampire cult who kidnapped Prince James baby in hopes of receiving immortality due to the child’s blood line.

Prince James of Albany

Actor: Simon Templeman

Sole descendent of the Stuart blood line. He hires Gabriel to protect and then rescue his son from night visitors who intend to kill the child.

Grace Nakimura

Actress: Charity James

Taking on a larger role in this game, Grace continues to assist Gabriel on his Schattenjager case. As the game progresses tensions between her and Gabriel come to a climax and cause her to make the choice of leaving at the end of the game.

Franklin Mosely

Actor: David Thomas

Secretly working for the CIA, Mosley works with Gabriel to try and find the lost treasure before anyone else on the tour group can. While also looking into secret societies located in the area.

Madeline Buthane

Actress: Jennifer Hale

Working as both the Tour guide for the treasure seekers and secretly as a French Internal Secret Service agent. She is looking into why an excavation has suddenly been ordered for the area.

John Wilkes

Actor: Richard Doyle

An Australian treasure seeker who is sure he will find the treasure first with the help of his geological maps of the area and his seismic instruments. He divulges this to Grace while drunk. That same night he is murdered by the vampire cult.

Lady Howard & Estelle Stiles

Actress: Samantha Eggar & Carolyn Sermour

British Actress and friend who are working together to find the treasure for themselves. With the help of a Dr.Wen’s research and their own copy of the infamous paper “Le Serpant Rouge” the feel they have the best chance of getting the treasure first.

Emilio Baza

Actor: Billy West

A fellow member of the treasure hunting group with a massive secret. In truth he is a former magi who was deeply devoted to the Jesus. After drink Jesus’ blood he became immortal, soon to his regret, and began walking the world endlessly.

Vittorio Buchelli

Actor: Joe Lala

Another member of the treasure hunting group. In truth Buchelli works as a Vatican Priest searching the area in an attempt to protect the catholic church.

Abbé Arnaud

Actor: Greg Berger

A local priest for the priory of sion. Gabriel suspects him for the kidnapping at first before realising Arnaud had nothing to do with the case.

Larry Chester

Actor: Corey Burton

A Scottish researcher and secret Masonic Brother writing a manuscript on the Knights templar and the blood lines of the area.
